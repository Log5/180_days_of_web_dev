Day 1 - Project Soft Skills

The 5 Most Sought-After Soft Skills

And 3 Actions To Improve Yourself

Creativity

  • Step 1: Tell yourself you are creative
  • Step 2: Start a creativity journal to track your ideas
  • Step 3: Use the "Six Hats" technique

Persuasion

  • Step 1: Understand the needs of the other party
  • Step 2: Find a common goal
  • Step 3: Show your passion and confidence

Collaboration

  • Step 1: Set clear goals and roles
  • Step 2: Be open-minded, learn to compromise
  • Step 3: Celebrate the small wins

Adaptability

  • Step 1: Force yourself to take risks
  • Step 2: Embrace learning
  • Step 3: Learn from others, ask questions

Emotional Intelligence

  • Step 1: Be motivated, maintain positive attitude
  • Step 2: Take critique well
  • Step 3: Listen and understand others
