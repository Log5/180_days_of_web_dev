Day 5 - Project Agile

What is Agile?

Agile is the ability to create and to respond to change in an uncertain and turbulent environment

Agile is a mindset.

Widely-used Agile methodologies include:


Scrum

Lean

Kanban

The general principles are

Satisfy the client

Concentrate on delivering frequently

Must be based on motivated people

Promote sustainable development

Regularly, the team will reflect on how to become more effective
